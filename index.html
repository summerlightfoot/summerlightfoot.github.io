What Do You See?

Hi there, my name is Summer and I am a recent college graduate who received my B.A. in International Studies, Photography, and Chinese language in New York City.

I grew up in the Hudson Valley in a small New York town called Pine Bush. I like working and collaborating with other students and photographers and like to document the lives of others. I aspire to use documentary photography along with research and to capture different groups of people.

I currently attend the Milano School of International Affairs, Management, and Urban Policy at The New School for my M.A. in International Affairs with a concentration in Conflict and Security. In the future I hope to study abroad and document the lives of women and minorities in China.
